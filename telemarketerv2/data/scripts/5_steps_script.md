Dealing With Gatekeepers

When dealing with Gatekeepers Use the ‘Law Of Averages’ method.
Below is the most efficient way I know of getting past gatekeepers. 

Maximum success - minimum time. 

There are 3 stages. 
The key is giving minimal information and remaining in control by pausing only after a question (GQ) in the 2nd & 3rd stages.
1/ Just give your 1st name (Typically 20% will put you through)
2/ relax /reason (GQ) (Typically 20% will put you through)
3/Create interest(shorter version) (GQ) (Typically 20% will put you through)
Sound natural. Try not to sound like a salesperson.


Gatekeeper Questions (GQ)  
Can I speak to the owner / or owner's name, please? (always use the name if you have)
Are they there?
Are they in?
Are they around?
Is the owner in / around today?
Are you the owner? AVOID the word ‘AVAILABLE’

Sounds more natural if you use a different question each time!! (stage 2 &3)


If they are not there do not waste time asking when they will be back. Just take their name if not got.
